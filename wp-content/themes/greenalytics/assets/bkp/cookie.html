Cookie Policy and Preferences

During your first visit to our website, you were asked to accept the use of cookies and similar technologies through a notification banner. These technologies are used for various purposes, including improving the functionality of the website and collecting data about your device and interactions with the website. We use servers hosted by third-party service providers, which may be located outside the European Economic Area, to gather website traffic information. For more information about cookies and similar technologies, you can visit www.allaboutcookies.org."

Functional cookies

Basic website functionality requires the use of certain cookies, which are always enabled. For example, when you close the cookie banner, a cookie is placed on your device to prevent the banner from appearing again when you visit the website later.

Performance cookies

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to gather information about how visitors use and interact with our website. This includes the number of visitors to the site, the pages or content with the most traffic, the location of the visitors, and how they found the website (such as through a search or a link on another website). We use this information to improve the website by prioritizing updates to the most popular content or functionality.

Content testing

If we are working on updates to our website, we occasionally display original content to some visitors and updated content to other visitors to help us assess which version best optimizes the user experience. When we do this, we drop a cookie on your device to remember which version of the page you were served.

Advertising cookies & Social media cookies

We may work with social media and advertising partners such as Facebook, Google, and LinkedIn, who may place cookies on your device when you visit our website. These cookies allow these third-party websites to remember that you have visited our website and/or shown interest in our products. As a result, when you visit these third-party websites or a website that displays ads from our online advertising platform, the ads displayed to you may be more relevant to your interests. If you click on these ads, they will redirect you back to our website. Our third-party partners may also provide us with information about the total number of views, shares, and click-throughs that these ads have received

Conversion tracking

To measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns and other website goals, we may use conversion pixels. These pixels trigger a short code that lets us know when you have clicked on a particular button on our website or reached a specific page, such as a thank you page after completing a form or downloading content. Unless otherwise specified, these cookies remain on your device even after you close your browser.
