Data Management

GreenAlytics will provide you with a better-informed decision and create value through analytics.

What do we collect?

In the context of an ESG (environmental, social, and governance) company, data management might involve collecting and organising information about the company's environmental and social impacts and its governance practices.
Proper data management is essential for a company as it helps ensure that the company has accurate and reliable information about its performance, which is necessary for making informed decisions and transparently communicating about its activities.

What will GreenAlytics do with your data?

This data is processed to assess the company's performance in these areas and to report on its sustainability efforts to stakeholders.
