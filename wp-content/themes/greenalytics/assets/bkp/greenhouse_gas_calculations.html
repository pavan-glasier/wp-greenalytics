What exactly is carbon accounting?

Carbon accounting involves accurately measuring a company's carbon footprint and identifying ways to reduce CO2 emissions. This process involves calculating all GHG emissions produced by a specific activity over the course of a year. By using carbon accounting, companies can effectively target and reduce their GHG emissions.
GreenAlytics offers a comprehensive and user-friendly carbon accounting platform that makes it easier for organizations to reduce their carbon footprint.
The platform transforms consumption and emissions data into tangible actions and helps track and manage carbon transactions and inventory with the same level of precision as financial transactions.
